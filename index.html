Pizza has transcended its Italian origins and found a home in cuisines all over the world. In the United States, it's a staple in most cities, with regional variations like New York-style and Detroit-style pizza. In Japan, you can find unique toppings like squid and mayonnaise. In India, there's the spicy and flavorful "paneer tikka" pizza. The diversity of pizza worldwide reflects the adaptability of this beloved food.
